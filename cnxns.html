type: website
status: deployed feb. 2025
tools/stack: html, css, javascript


why

there was a phase where my friends and i were obsessed with nyt connections. i thought it would be fun to create a website to host connections puzzles that i'd designed. while the landing page's connections isn't specific to any of my friends (it's actually my interests :p), the site primarily exists to host pages with connections puzzles themed to specific friends, that i could send to friends individually.


what

features:
  • randomized ordering of connections words
  • count of incorrect guesses
  • notes correctly identified categories and removes the words from the puzzle
  • "hidden" pages (but still public for ease of access) for individual friends' connections puzzles

how

This was my first attempt at true vibecoding, as I'd never had html/css/js experience before. Built through a combination of free ChatGPT prompting and manual coding/debugging. Key tools were VSCode, ChatGPT, Github, terminal, and of course Google.

reflections

This project had the simple goal of hosting diy connections games that I could "gift" to friends. Indeed, it turned out quite simple - and it does the job. I was really pleasantly surprised by how AI has made coding so easy, especially for beginner projects, as someone who had no prior web dev experience. ChatGPT was also an excellent (if sometimes bullshitty) teacher, as it's like a constant 24/7 tutor who will answer your questions no matter what (even if it has to make stuff up), and all without the gatekeeping-ness of a stackoverflow commenter.

If I were to iterate further on this project, I think it would be interesting to figure out how create a page where web users could submit their connections puzzle, and the site would generate the UI-version. With that said, I mainly just wanted a web app to host personalized connections to gift to friends, and it's unlikely I'll iterate any further on this site any more than just adding more connections for friends.

If you do have a connections you'd like to add though, I'd be happy to host it on here :D
