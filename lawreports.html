why

what

overall features:
  • linking: linking of files for context
  • speech-to-text & text-to-speech: communicate via text or voice with agents
  • agent creation: add new agents, with designated roles
  • model selection: pi's agent harness offers access to hundreds of models, including from Anthropic and OpenAI
  • team chat: summaries of all agent responses & chats are collated in team chat sidebar
  • relay toggle: all summaries are relayed to all agents for context, but this toggle can also be turned off

how

For the UI, I heavily relied on Github Copilot in VScode for the workflow here, and most of all on my awesome team members. This was a super cool learning experience where I got to learn about wireframing and owning UI design, while being able to learn a ton and get a glimpse into the coding of my team members. Honestly, huge shoutouts to Binam and Robin, who I literally just met that day, and yet they took the time to answer every question I had and show me how to set up various agentic coding workflows. In terms of the real "how" for this app - ask the software engineers on the team :)

reflections

This was an incredibly fun project - and also my first hackathon! I'd only gotten 1.5 hours of sleep the night before, having flown back in from visiting family (and had procrastinated packing), but I knew I wanted make it to the last Boston Tech Week event after having missed most of it - and I'm so glad I did. This was a one-day hackathon on Sunday, where I got to meet so many amazing people, and especially this amazing team. It also really opened my world to the possibilities of agentic coding - the demos were incredible, and all coded in less than 10 hours. There were fully operational apps for everything from safe agentic commerce to travel apps with self-guided tours. Definitely left me with a wave of AI existentialism but also an insane amount of excitement for what's possible.

Looking ahead - I hope to improve my knowledge of agents and coding to a level where I'd be able to hack something like this from beginning to end - one day. Super grateful to have had this experience and most of all to have been able to work with my team on this.
